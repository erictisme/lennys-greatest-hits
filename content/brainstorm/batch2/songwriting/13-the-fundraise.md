# Smile Through the Silence
Album: The Startup Life
Genre: Piano-driven indie folk-rock
Mood: Intimate, building, bittersweet
BPM: 108
Key: Dm
Reference: The National (baritone intimacy, restrained tension), Bon Iver (raw vulnerability), The War on Drugs (building momentum)
Source: "A playbook for fundraising" by Marc McCabe — Lenny's Newsletter
Source Date: 2020-09-01
Topics: fundraising, rejection, founder loneliness, venture capital, persistence
JTBD Category: Starting and scaling a startup
Chord Progression: Dm-F-C-Am | Bb-F-C-Dm | Gm-Dm-Bb-A

## Chord Notes
- **Verse (Dm - F - C - Am):** D minor opens with tension and uncertainty. The walk up to F gives a moment of breath, C opens the sky briefly, Am pulls you back down. Restless, searching — matches the founder grinding alone at the kitchen table.
- **Chorus (Bb - F - C - Dm):** The Bb major lifts the chorus above the verse — reaching for something bigger. The fall back to Dm on the final line lands with the weight of waiting. Hopeful and heavy at once.
- **Bridge (Gm - Dm - Bb - A):** Gm darkens for the turn, Dm holds tension, Bb lifts, then A major (the dominant) creates the biggest emotional pivot — the breakthrough moment. A major is the light breaking through.

## Killer Line
"They do this every Tuesday / You do this once in your life"

## Lyrics

[Verse 1]
Four weeks carving slides at two AM
Pitching to the mirror, start again
Eight months runway melting in your hands
Six friends nod and smile — they're being kind

[Chorus]
They do this every Tuesday
You do this once in your life
Smile through the silence, play the part
Sell the dream that's tearing you apart

[Verse 2]
Five pitches a day for two straight weeks
Warm handshakes and then the silence creeps
Can't tell the team the walls are closing in
Rehearse the smile and walk back in

[Chorus]
They do this every Tuesday
You do this once in your life
Hold the line and tell them you're fine
Pour your heart out one more time

[Bridge]
Then one leans in and asks about the dream
Not the deck — the thing behind the screen
They fight for you in rooms you'll never see
The email drops — "We'd like to lead..."

[Final Chorus]
They did this every Tuesday
Someone believed before you did
Pop the cork and catch your breath
The hard part starts again tonight

[Outro]
You celebrate, you breathe, you start the climb
The fundraise never ends
