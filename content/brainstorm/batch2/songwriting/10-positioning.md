You ain't that — now they see you and they buy

[Outro]
You don't know what you are

## Chord Progressions

**Verse: G - Bm7 - C - D**
The Bm7 adds soulful tension underneath the storytelling. The progression moves forward steadily, like April working through her call list — patient but building toward revelation.

**Chorus: C - D - G - Em**
Lifts from the verse with C-D creating an ascending push into the G resolution. The Em at the end keeps it from fully resolving — because "you ain't that" is a challenge, not a conclusion.

**Bridge: Am7 - D7 - Gmaj7 - Em**
Jazz-influenced voicings for the reflective moment. The Gmaj7 adds warmth to "you're just badly introduced" and the Em leaves space before the final chorus hits with full confidence.
