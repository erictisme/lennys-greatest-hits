## The ONE Thing
Never give them your number first — the game has rules, and once you learn them, you stop leaving money on the table.

## Killer Line
"They built this game to keep you quiet"

## Chord Progressions

**Verse: Bbmaj7 → Gm7 → Ebmaj7 → F7**
Warm and contemplative — the Gm7 carries the anxiety of the negotiation moment. Classic soul movement that feels like sitting with a knot in your stomach. The Bbmaj7 opening is inviting, then Gm7 drops the floor out.

**Chorus: Eb → Bb/D → Cm7 → F**
Gospel-inflected IV–I/3–ii–V. The Bb/D walking bass gives the upward, empowering lift. Each repetition builds confidence — from quiet defiance to full anthem. The F dominant creates a satisfying pull back to the next section.

**Bridge: Gm7 → Eb → F → Bb**
Starting on vi (minor) for the darker "they want you small" reveal, then resolving to Bb major for the empowerment turn. The F→Bb is a strong V→I resolution — the moment clarity arrives and the anger becomes power.
