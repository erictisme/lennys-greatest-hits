## Killer Line
"Not stuck down here, I'm stuck going up"

## Chord Progression Notes
- **Verse:** Em9 - Am9 - D9 - Gmaj9 — Minor-colored jazz-funk voicings for the uncertainty of being stuck, resolving to major for a glimmer of hope. Rhodes electric piano territory.
- **Chorus:** Gmaj7 - D/F# - Em7 - Cmaj7 — Rising then falling bass line mirrors the loop's circular motion. Bright, open, cyclical.
- **Bridge:** Cm9 - F13 - Bbmaj7 - Eb9 — Borrowed chords from G minor create dramatic tension for the "stuck going up" moment, before resolving back to G for the final chorus.
