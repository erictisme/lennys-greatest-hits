## Chord Notes
- **Verse (Gmaj7 → Em9 → Cmaj7 → D7):** Warm jazz-pop movement (I-vi-IV-V). The major 7ths keep it smooth and reflective — simmering frustration, not a boil. Em9 carries the melancholy of feeling unseen.
- **Chorus (Cmaj7 → D/F# → Em7 → Gmaj7):** Ascending bass line (IV-V-vi-I) lifts into brightness. Walking bass up to G feels like stepping into the light — you're taking control.
- **Bridge (Am7 → Bm7 → Cmaj7 → D7sus4):** Stepwise climb (ii-iii-IV-V) builds tension for the tough-love dialogue. The sus4 hangs unresolved before releasing into the final chorus.

## Killer Line
"They can't reward the work they've never seen"
