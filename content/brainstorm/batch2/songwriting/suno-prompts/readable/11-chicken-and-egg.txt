TITLE: Nobody's Coming

STYLE: driving garage rock, raw distorted electric guitar riffs with fuzz, punchy drums with floor-tom fills, gritty bass locked to kick drum, energetic male vocal with punk urgency and heart, close-mic vocals with slight room reverb, 148 BPM, key of E major, reference Arctic Monkeys AM-era urgency, reference The Black Keys Brothers-era raw garage tone, reference The Strokes cool detached vocal delivery, builds from restrained verse to explosive chorus, scrappy triumphant energy, anthemic final chorus belted wide, studio quality with live-room warmth

SOURCE: How to Kickstart and Scale a Marketplace Business (8-part series) (Lenny Rachitsky + 25 marketplace leaders, newsletter)

[Verse 1]

Two empty sides, both the same

So we showed up at the door

Someone went first through the door
