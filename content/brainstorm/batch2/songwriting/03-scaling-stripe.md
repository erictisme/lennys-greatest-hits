## Chord Progression Notes

**Verse: E - B/D# - C#m - A** (I - V/VII - vi - IV)
Descending bass line (E→D#→C#→A) creates forward momentum — the feeling of someone assessing a situation and building toward action. Mirrors Claire walking in and running the numbers.

**Chorus: A - E/G# - B - C#m** (IV - I - V - vi)
Classic anthem lift. Starting on IV gives immediate emotional elevation. The vi at the end creates tension that drives back to IV on repeat — keeps the chorus cycling with relentless energy, like a machine that doesn't stop.

**Bridge: C#m - A - E - B** (vi - IV - I - V)
Starting on the relative minor shifts into vulnerability — the invisible operator finally gets her spotlight. The progression resolves upward through I to V, matching the emotional arc from difficulty to purpose. The open V at the end sets up the final chorus with maximum lift.
