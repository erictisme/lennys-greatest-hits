## Thin Albums — What's Missing

### The Talent Search (4 tracks) — needs 4+ more

Current coverage: first hires, executive recruiting, interview questions, hiring playbook.

**Missing topics:**
- Reference checking / backdoor references
- Building diverse teams / reducing bias in hiring
- Hiring for culture add vs. culture fit
- When to hire senior vs. grow junior talent
- Comp strategy / equity negotiation from the employer side

**Potential sources from uncovered episodes:**
- Jessica Livingston — evaluating founders (social radar) could adapt to evaluating candidates
- Deb Liu — building diverse leadership teams

### By the Numbers (6 tracks) — needs 2+ more

Current coverage: retention, cohort analysis, activation metrics, free-to-paid, customer obsession, subscription value loop.

**Missing topics:**
- NPS / customer satisfaction measurement
- Revenue metrics (LTV, CAC, payback period)
- Marketplace metrics (liquidity, GMV, take rate) — partially in Growth Engine's `the-liquidity`
- Experiment velocity / shipping speed metrics

**Potential sources from uncovered episodes:**
- Sean Ellis — growth metrics, the "very disappointed" PMF survey metric
- Bangaly Kaba — unorthodox growth metrics from YouTube/Instagram

### Deep Work (6 tracks) — needs 2+ more

Current coverage: prioritization (LNO), focus, time management, engineering mindset, decision-making.

**Missing topics:**
- Deep work rituals / environment design
- Saying no to meetings / protecting maker schedules
- Systems thinking / second-order effects
- Writing as thinking (memo culture, Amazon-style narratives)

**Potential sources from uncovered episodes:**
- Camille Fournier — engineering workflows, manager vs. maker schedules
- Boz Bosworth — how Meta runs at scale while protecting focus

### The Interview (5 tracks) — needs 3+ more

Current coverage: salary negotiation, job search councils, first-round interviews, product sense interviews, portfolio careers.

**Missing topics:**
- Behavioral interview prep (STAR method, storytelling)
- System design / case study interviews
- Evaluating companies (what to look for in your next role)
- Handling rejection / interview resilience
- Networking without being slimy

**Potential sources from uncovered episodes:**
- Deb Liu — career transitions, evaluating new roles
- Nikhyl Singhal (already covered in Career Moves) — could extend to interview context

## Recommendations for Batch 8

1. **Priority: Fill The Talent Search** (4 tracks → 8). It's the thinnest album and hiring is one of Lenny's most-covered topics.
2. **Secondary: Fill The Interview** (5 → 8). Job searching is high-interest for Lenny's audience.
3. **Tertiary: Fill By the Numbers and Deep Work** (6 → 8 each). Metrics and productivity are core Lenny themes.
4. **Avoid adding to The Startup Life or The Craft** — both are already heavy at 16-17 tracks.
5. **Total songs needed for balance:** ~12 songs to bring all thin albums to 8 tracks.
