# Research Brief: How Have I Been Complicit — Jerry Colonna

**Source:** Lenny's Podcast — Jerry Colonna interview (May 8, 2025)
**Album:** The Human Side

---

## Story Arc

**Beginning:** Jerry names the lie at the center of startup culture — "all our companies are moving up and to the right, every product is working — and that's just a lie." He introduces the complicit question as a tool for cutting through.

**Turning Point:** He turns the lens directly on Lenny in real time — asking why he's busy, probing the "little whispery voice" that says "they might find out," exposing that busyness is a mechanism to quiet imposter syndrome. It becomes a live coaching session.

**End:** Legacy — what kind of ancestor do you want to be? Jerry's toppled oak tree as himself, dissolving slowly into earth after sheltering others. Better humans make better leaders.

---

- Jerry has been coaching for 27 years

## Emotional Core

The gap between what you project and what you actually feel. The loneliness of performing success while suffering underneath. The relief when someone says "you don't have to bullshit here." We are trained from childhood to equate achievement with worth.

---

## The ONE Thing

The conditions you say you don't want — you built them. Not because you're broken, but because you weren't awake. Wake up.
