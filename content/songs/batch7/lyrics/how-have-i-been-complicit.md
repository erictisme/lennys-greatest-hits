# How Have I Been Complicit

## Metadata
- **Source:** Lenny's Podcast — Jerry Colonna (May 8, 2025)
- **Album:** The Human Side
- **Genre:** Intimate acoustic folk
- **Key:** C#m (Dbm)
- **BPM:** 72
- **Time Signature:** 4/4
- **Vocal:** Warm male baritone — whispered verses, slightly more open chorus. Close-mic, room ambience.
- **Production:** Voice and acoustic guitar only. No drums. No bass. No production. Silence is an instrument.
- **JTBD:** For the founder who keeps a packed calendar and wonders why they're still exhausted
- **Killer Line:** "I didn't pull the trigger but I drove the car again"
