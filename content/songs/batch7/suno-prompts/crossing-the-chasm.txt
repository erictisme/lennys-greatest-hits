TITLE: Crossing the Chasm
STYLE: Folk-rock, 12/8 shuffle feel, 108 BPM, Gb major. Warm male vocal — conversational in verses, building to anthemic in chorus. Acoustic guitar and upright bass drive the shuffle groove. Muted trumpet enters on the bridge with Chet Baker warmth and quiet urgency — unhurried, human. Full band swells into the final chorus with trumpet carrying the melody. Production is intimate but not small — think early Mumford & Sons meets Bill Withers. No overproduction, no electric distortion. The kindling metaphor should feel lived-in, not clever.
SOURCE: Lenny's Podcast — Geoffrey Moore interview
---
