TITLE: The Long Game
STYLE: Acoustic soul waltz, 3/4 time, 78 BPM, F# major. Warm male tenor vocal, intimate close-mic delivery, breathy and unhurried. Fingerpicked acoustic guitar carries verse 1. Cello enters quietly at verse 2 — warm, bowed, low register — and becomes the emotional anchor through the rest of the song. Upright bass, felt not heard. Brushed minimal percussion (brush on snare, no kick). Rhodes chord fills in the bridge. Warm analog production, slight vinyl room ambience. References: Jon Guerra intimacy, Jonathan Ogden close-mic warmth, Iron and Wine cinematic specificity. Song should feel like 2am and a long career behind you.
SOURCE: Lenny's Podcast — "Building a long and meaningful career | Nikhyl Singhal (Meta, Google)" (June 11, 2023)
---
