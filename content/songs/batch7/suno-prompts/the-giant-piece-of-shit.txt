TITLE: The Giant Piece of Shit

STYLE: Indie folk-rock anthem, 100 BPM, Bb major, 4/4. Warm male baritone vocal — intimate and conversational in verses, rising to a belted, emotionally raw chorus. Think The National meets early Wilco. Acoustic guitar drives verses with fingerpicked arpeggios. Electric guitar enters at pre-chorus, full band (drums, bass, electric, keys) hits on chorus downbeat. Bridge strips back to acoustic guitar and voice before final chorus explodes. Unique sonic element: audience chant at outro — call and response, voices building to a crowd singing "The measure of our success / will be the customer's success." Slow fade on chant. No distortion. No aggression. Emotional weight through restraint. Melancholy but determined. Dark warmth.

SOURCE: Stewart Butterfield MIT Technology Review interview, 2014 (Podcast, Stewart Butterfield)
