TITLE: Thinking in Bets
STYLE: Jazz-pop, neo-soul undertones. Rhodes/Wurlitzer piano-driven, warm upright bass, brushed drums, no guitar. Sultry breathy female vocal, jazz-pop delivery — confident but intimate, Norah Jones meets Esperanza Spalding. Swing feel throughout. Eb major, 92 BPM. Late-night mood, cerebral and warm. Chord flavor: jazz voicings — maj7, m9, 13th chords. No bright synths. Vinyl warmth, close-mic vocal.
SOURCE: "Thinking in Bets" with Annie Duke, Lenny's Podcast
---
