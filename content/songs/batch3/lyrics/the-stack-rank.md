## Quality Critique
- **Sing Test:** "Put it on the stack rank" is a strong, rhythmic hook. Pre-chorus drives momentum. Open vowels on "go," "no," "know," "close."
- **Blog Test:** No definitions or thesis statements. V1 tells Tara's "do both" story as a scene. V2 dramatizes the Bezos screws story. Bridge uses the Billboard lyrics anecdote.
- **Universal Test:** Works for anyone who's been overloaded and forced to choose. The emotional arc from overwhelm to liberation is universal.
- **Filler Test:** Every line earned. Bridge is the most packed section but every detail comes from the source.
- **Chorus Test:** Three variations — "quiet no," "someone else's slow," "a future you let go" — each deepens the emotional stakes. Final chorus lands on freedom/release.
