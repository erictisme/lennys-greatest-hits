## Critique Notes
- Sing Test: Read aloud — groovy, the pre-chorus has a tight call-and-response feel. "Pass it down and build it up" has natural rhythm for neo-soul pocket.
- Blog Test: No thesis statements. "That's a white flag, not a plan" is character voice/scene, not definition. "Arguing the shade of wall" is concrete imagery for alignment dysfunction.
- Universal Test: Planning season dread is universal. No insider terms — "the W" is implied through imagery (down/up pattern) without naming the framework.
- Filler Test: Every line either paints a scene (March drawing lines, bags packed, shade of wall) or advances the emotional arc.
- Chorus Test: Three choruses vary — "before we run" → "before we cut" → "stay on the track." Final line shifts from "nothing to prove" → "no one second-guessed" → "and that's a lot." Arc from confidence to pride.
