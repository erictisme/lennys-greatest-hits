## Chord Progressions

### Verse: Dm - Gm - Bb - A7 (i - iv - VI - V7)
Minor key sets the honest, slightly restless mood. Dm opens with introspection, Gm deepens the pull, Bb opens up warmth, A7 creates gentle tension that resolves back. The loop mirrors the cycle of distraction — always returning to the same desk.

### Chorus: Gm - A7 - Dm - F (iv - V7 - i - III)
Starts on the searching Gm, A7 builds anticipation, resolves to Dm with quiet certainty ("this feeling fades"). The F at the end lifts slightly — a glimpse of the major, the hope that sitting with it works.

### Bridge: Bb - Gm - A7 - Dm (VI - iv - V7 - i)
Opens wide on Bb for the etymology revelation (traction/distraction), narrows through Gm and A7, lands firmly on Dm. The "you're not broken" line sits on the resolution — grounded, assured.
