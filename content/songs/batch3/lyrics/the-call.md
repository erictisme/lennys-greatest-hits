## Critique Notes
- Sing Test: Read aloud — flows naturally, open vowels on "call," "wall," "all," "fall." Chorus is immediately hummable.
- Blog Test: No thesis statements. "Consensus is a hiding place" is imagery, not definition. "Not 'cause I'm right, I might be wrong" is character voice.
- Universal Test: Anyone who's been in a room where nobody would decide gets this. No Lenny-specific jargon.
- Filler Test: Every line advances the scene or emotion. No throwaway rhymes.
- Chorus Test: Each chorus changes — "walk through anyway" → "writing out" / "own the fall" → "every consequence that falls." Emotional arc from courage to accountability.
