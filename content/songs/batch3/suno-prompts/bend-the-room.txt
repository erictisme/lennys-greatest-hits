TITLE: Bend the Room

STYLE: Jazz-pop groove with FINGER SNAPS as the rhythmic backbone — snaps carry the whole verse, drums don't enter until chorus. Clean electric guitar with chorus effect, warm Rhodes piano. BPM ~114, key of F# minor. Warm male vocal, smooth swagger, conversational and knowing. The snaps represent the soft influence — no force, just rhythm pulling you in. Chorus: brushed drums enter with shaker, groove opens up. Bridge: snaps alone with Rhodes. Tom Misch sophistication meets Stephen Day warmth. The groove IS the persuasion.

SOURCE: "A PM's guide to influence" by Jules Walter + "How to get better at influence" by Lenny Rachitsky

---
