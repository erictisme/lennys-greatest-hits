TITLE: Bend the Room

STYLE: Tom Misch jazz-pop groove, clean electric guitar with chorus effect, warm Rhodes piano, brushed drums with light shaker. BPM ~104, Eb major. Warm male vocal, confident but not aggressive — smooth swagger with intimate delivery. Close-mic, lo-fi warmth. Upright bass, subtle finger snaps. Think Tom Misch meets Stephen Day — sophisticated but accessible, groove-forward without being busy.

SOURCE: "A PM's guide to influence" by Jules Walter + "How to get better at influence" by Lenny Rachitsky

---
