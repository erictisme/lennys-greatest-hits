TITLE: The Plan

STYLE: Neo-soul groove with jazz-pop sophistication. Jacob Collier harmonic warmth meets Tom Misch guitar pocket. Warm Rhodes/Wurlitzer chords, clean electric guitar with subtle wah, brushed drums in a relaxed pocket, round upright bass. Close-mic warm male vocal, confident and earnest, not rushed. BPM ~98, key of Eb major. Stephen Day indie sincerity. Half-time bridge drops to intimate whisper before building back. Lo-fi warmth, vinyl texture, room feel.

SOURCE: "The Secret to a Great Planning Process — Lessons from Airbnb and Eventbrite" (Lenny's Newsletter / First Round Review)

---
