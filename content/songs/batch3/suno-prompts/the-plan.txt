TITLE: The Plan

STYLE: Neo-soul groove with HAND CLAPS driving the rhythm — the team moving together. 6/8 feel, BPM ~98, key of Gb major. Warm Wurlitzer chords, clean electric guitar with subtle wah, round upright bass. Hand claps on beat 1 and 4, snapping fingers on off-beats — the groove is PEOPLE, not drums. Close-mic warm male vocal, confident and earnest. Bridge drops to half-time whisper, claps fade to a single person clapping, then builds back as voices layer in. Jacob Collier harmonic warmth. The rhythm section IS the team aligning.

SOURCE: "The Secret to a Great Planning Process — Lessons from Airbnb and Eventbrite" (Lenny's Newsletter / First Round Review)

---
