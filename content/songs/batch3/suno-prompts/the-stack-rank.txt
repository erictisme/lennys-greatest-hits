TITLE: The Stack Rank

STYLE: Gentle indie rock, The 1975 soft synth-pop side meets Stephen Day warmth. BPM ~108, Ab major. Warm synth pad, intimate piano, light brushed drums building to full arrangement by final chorus. Warm male vocal, earnest and forward-moving without aggression. Close-mic delivery, lo-fi warmth. Bass guitar (warm, round, felt not heard). Think "Somebody Else" era 1975 softness crossed with Stephen Day sincerity — emotional momentum that builds to cathartic release.

SOURCE: "How to communicate tradeoffs so leaders will listen" by Tara Seshan

---
