TITLE: The Stack Rank

STYLE: Indie rock with building intensity — starts stripped, ends CATHARTIC. The 1975 "Somebody Else" soft energy building to Hollow Coves cinematic release. BPM ~120, key of Ab major. Verse 1: intimate piano + voice ONLY. Verse 2: synth pad enters, bass guitar felt underneath. Chorus: brushed drums, warm synth layers. Bridge: the Tara Seshan "Billboard chart" moment — playful, almost laughing. Final chorus: FULL arrangement, driving drums (not brushed — full kit), layered vocals, the cathartic release of finally letting go of scope. The dynamic range IS the stack rank — stripping away until only the essential remains, then celebrating it.

SOURCE: "How to communicate tradeoffs so leaders will listen" by Tara Seshan

---
