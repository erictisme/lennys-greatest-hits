TITLE: Ninety-Three Percent

STYLE: Boom-bap hip hop with vinyl DJ scratches as signature texture. Harder drums than lo-fi — crisp snare hits, punchy kick, DJ scratch accents on transitions. BPM 94, key of Bb minor. Warm Rhodes keys, upright bass. Male vocal — confident spoken flow on verses, melodic hook on chorus. Call-and-response chorus: main voice calls, layered voices respond. Vinyl crackle + TURNTABLE SCRATCH sounds between sections. Think J Dilla drum pocket meets Loyle Carner storytelling. Energy: 3AM prep session turning into "I got this" swagger. More confident and rhythmic than lo-fi — this is someone who's READY.

SOURCE: How to pass any first-round interview (even in a terrible talent market) (Coach Erika, Newsletter)

---
