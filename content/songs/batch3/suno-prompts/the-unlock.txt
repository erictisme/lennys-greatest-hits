TITLE: The Unlock

STYLE: Dream-pop lo-fi with REVERSE REVERB guitar swells as signature texture — sounds appearing backwards then resolving forward, like unlocking something hidden. Hazy guitar arpeggios, soft electric piano, gentle bass groove. BPM 96, key of Db major. Breathy FEMALE vocal with occasional vocal layering, half-sung half-spoken, intimate and curious. Japanese Breakfast meets Doris Club. Brushed drums, lo-fi vinyl warmth. The reverse reverb swells happen at the start of each chorus — the unlock moment made sonic. Bridge: everything goes ambient and spacious before snapping back.

SOURCE: What is a good activation rate / How to determine your activation metric (Lenny's Newsletter)
