TITLE: Never Search Alone

STYLE: Acoustic soul with warm strings building to communal swell. Iron and Wine intimacy meets Chet Baker cool. Warm male tenor, melismatic phrasing, breathy and soulful, close-mic. Nylon string guitar, upright bass, Rhodes piano, brushed drums with shaker. BPM 78, key of Db major. What makes this different: WARM STRINGS enter at chorus 2 — violin and cello playing long sustained notes, communal and hopeful. Bridge: stripped to voice + nylon guitar. Final chorus: full string arrangement swells underneath the vocal — the community literally grows in the arrangement. Lo-fi vinyl warmth, room ambience. The strings represent the people showing up.

SOURCE: Land your dream job in today's market: negotiation tactics, job search councils, and more (Phyl Terry, Podcast)

---
