TITLE: The New Stack

STYLE: Lo-fi electronic synth-folk with VOCODER vocal texture on bridge — the AI theme made sonic. Doris Club dreamy textures meets The 1975 soft synth-pop. Warm analog synth pads, gentle electronic pulse, programmed beat. BPM ~95, key of G# minor. Close-mic breathy male vocal (Stephen Day warmth), nylon guitar layered with Rhodes keys. Bridge: voice runs through warm vocoder/harmonizer creating layered harmony — the "machine" singing with the human. Final chorus: human voice returns clean, triumphant over the synths. Vinyl texture, contemplative but forward-moving.

SOURCE: Counterintuitive advice for building AI products (Lenny Rachitsky & Kyle Poyar, newsletter)
