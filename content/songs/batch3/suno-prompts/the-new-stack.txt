TITLE: The New Stack

STYLE: Lo-fi electronic synth-folk, Doris Club dreamy textures meets The 1975 soft synth-pop. Warm analog synth pads, gentle electronic pulse, brushed percussion with programmed beat underneath, close-mic breathy male vocal (Stephen Day warmth), nylon guitar layered with Rhodes keys, vinyl texture, room ambience. BPM ~95, key of Eb major. Mood: contemplative but forward-moving, the future feels warm not cold. Builds gently from sparse verse to full chorus with layered synths. Outro fades with reverb.

SOURCE: Counterintuitive advice for building AI products (Lenny Rachitsky & Kyle Poyar, newsletter)

---
