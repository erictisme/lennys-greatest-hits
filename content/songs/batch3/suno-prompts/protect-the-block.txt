TITLE: Protect the Block

STYLE: Minimal ambient electronic. Warm analog synth pad, gentle programmed beat (not acoustic drums), soft side-chain pulse. NO acoustic guitar. Warm male vocal, half-whispered, close-mic. Clean electric piano (not Rhodes — think Nils Frahm felt piano). BPM ~90, key of F# major. Subtle bass synth, barely there. Verse 1 is voice + synth pad only. Beat enters at chorus. Bridge drops to pure ambient texture + whisper. Final chorus: full arrangement with layered vocal harmonies. Think James Blake gentle side meets Bon Iver "22, A Million" warmth. The stillness IS the production.

SOURCE: "Time management techniques that actually work" Parts 1 & 2 (Lenny's Newsletter)

---
