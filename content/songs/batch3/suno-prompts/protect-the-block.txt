TITLE: Protect the Block

STYLE: Acoustic folk, Jon Guerra contemplative intensity. Fingerpicked acoustic guitar, gentle shaker percussion, warm upright bass. Warm male baritone vocal, intimate and reflective, close-mic with room ambience. BPM ~84, key of G major. Honest and unhurried, like a quiet morning before the world wakes up. Lo-fi warmth, vinyl texture.

SOURCE: "Time management techniques that actually work" Parts 1 & 2 (Lenny's Newsletter)

---

LYRICS:

[Intro, fingerpicked acoustic]

[Verse 1]
Phone in the other room
Notebook open, page is new
Three things written, nothing more
The morning knows what it's for
Headphones on, the binaural hum
Coffee warm, the screen goes numb
Thirty minutes till the deep sets in
Then the world outside gets thin

[Chorus]
Protect the block, protect the block
This hour is not for sale
Every yes you give away
Is a no to something real
Guard the morning, guard the space
Let the inbox wait its turn
The door is closed, the work is here
This is how the good things burn

[Verse 2]
Sunday night, fifteen minutes
Scan the week before it starts
Slot the hard things in the gaps
Tear the old page, fresh new marks
Would you say yes if it's tomorrow?
Feel the weight of that quick thought
Half your days are someone else's
Meetings built on things you bought

[Chorus]
Protect the block, protect the block
This hour is not for sale
Every yes you give away
Is a no you'll always feel
Guard the silence, guard the depth
Let the calendar hold firm
The door is closed, the flow is here
This is where the best things turn

[Bridge, whispered]
It's not the meeting on its own
It's the hour before you can't go deep
And the hour after, rebuilding
What you lost when you broke the keep

[Chorus, building warmth]
Protect the block, protect the block
Your time is not for sale
Every no becomes a gift
To the work that tips the scale
Guard the morning, guard the craft
Let the rest of it go still
The door is closed, the pen is down
And the best work always will

[Outro, gentle]
Protect the block...
The door is closed...
