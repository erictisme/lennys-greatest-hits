TITLE: Measure Twice

STYLE: Jazz-pop with muted trumpet accents. 82 BPM, key of Dm. Warm Rhodes piano, upright bass walking gently, brushed drums in a pocket groove. Muted trumpet plays short answering phrases between vocal lines (Chet Baker style — cool, not flashy). Close-mic warm male tenor, conversational and unhurried. Tom Misch sophistication. NO electric guitar. Trumpet featured in bridge — solo over Rhodes and bass, patient and beautiful. Lo-fi analog warmth, intimate room ambience, 2AM studio session feel.

SOURCE: How to measure cohort retention / How to increase your product's retention (Lenny's Newsletter)

---

Every column tells me what I seek

I've been calling things "active"
