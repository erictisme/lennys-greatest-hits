TITLE: The Closer

STYLE: Neo-soul groove with ALTO SAXOPHONE as signature instrument. Sax plays smooth answering phrases between vocal lines — think late-night recruiting dinner energy. Tom Misch clean electric guitar, warm Rhodes chords, groovy fingerstyle bass. BPM ~100, key of Db major. Male vocal — smooth and confident, Chet Baker cool delivery. Finger snaps and rim clicks, minimal drums. Sax solo in bridge — warm, unhurried, seductive. Jacob Collier harmonic extensions (9ths, 13ths). The sax makes this song feel like a handshake deal over whiskey.

SOURCE: Lessons from one of the world's top executive recruiters (Lauren Ipsen, podcast)

---
