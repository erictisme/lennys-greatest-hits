TITLE: The Call

STYLE: Folk-rock with stomp-clap rhythm and building strings. 12/8 shuffle feel, BPM ~96, key of Bbm. Opens with hand claps and foot stomps setting the rhythm — urgent, like a heartbeat. Acoustic guitar enters with driving strum pattern (not fingerpicked). Warm male vocal, starting controlled, building to commanding delivery. Chorus: full band with driving drums (not brushed — full kit, purposeful), electric guitar with edge, violin adding urgency. Bridge: everything drops to voice alone, whispered. Then STRINGS build from silence — cello first, then violin, then full arrangement crashes back for final chorus. Congregational energy in final chorus — room of voices. Hollow Coves cinematic build meets Mumford & Sons rhythmic drive. This song is about making the hard call — it should FEEL like stepping forward.

SOURCE: "My favorite decision-making frameworks" (Lenny's Newsletter)
