TITLE: The Obsession

STYLE: Jazz-pop, F# minor, 92 BPM, 4/4 time. Sultry female vocal — intimate, dry-witted, jazz-inflected, in the register of Norah Jones warmth crossed with Phoebe Bridgers restraint. The rhythmic spine is a walking upright bass that never stops — the obsession never stops. Rhodes piano carries the verse melody, sparse and precise. Brushed drums, very light — more texture than rhythm. Subtle string swells on the bridge only. No guitar. No percussion fills. Space is the production choice. The song feels like someone thinking aloud in a late-night office, watching a dashboard tick upward in the dark.

SOURCE: Jeff Weinstein (Lenny's Podcast)
