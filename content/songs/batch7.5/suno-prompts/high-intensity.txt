TITLE: High Intensity

STYLE: Upbeat indie rock, folk-rock energy, 124 BPM, B major, 4/4 time. Male vocal — energetic, slightly punchy, confident and clear, Vampire Weekend meets Phoenix. Clean electric guitar (bright, jangly, not distorted), hand claps as the primary rhythmic drive (clap-along builders' anthem feel), punchy bass guitar, bright piano/keys accents, tight full drum kit (snappy snare, not heavy). Production is crisp and clean — no reverb wash, no stadium reverb. Think early Vampire Weekend (Mansard Roof era) meets Phoenix (Lisztomania). Pre-chorus drops slightly to set up the chorus explosion. Bridge is more stripped — just voice, hand claps, and bass — then full band crashes back for the final chorus. The outro slows just slightly, more intimate, like the band winding down after a great show.

SOURCE: Farhan Thawar & Tobi Lütke (Lenny's Podcast)
