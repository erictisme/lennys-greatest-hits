TITLE: The Value Loop

STYLE: Lo-fi neo-soul, Eb major, 96 BPM, 4/4 time. Warm male vocal — conversational, intimate, slightly worn. Produced like a late-night recording session. A Rhodes piano riff loops throughout the entire track without fully resolving — the musical metaphor for the subscription loop itself. Programmed lo-fi drums with a steady hi-hat pattern that never drops, like a clock ticking. Vinyl crackle texture underneath everything. Warm synth pad in the background. Bass guitar walking a circular pattern. The groove never stops — it fades but doesn't conclude. Think Cautious Clay meets Rex Orange County meets Leon Bridges. Sparse arrangement — every instrument earns its place. Mid-tempo, deliberate. Not anxious, not triumphant. The feeling of a machine finding its rhythm.

SOURCE: Phil Carter — The Subscription Value Loop (Lenny's Newsletter, Sep 3, 2024)
