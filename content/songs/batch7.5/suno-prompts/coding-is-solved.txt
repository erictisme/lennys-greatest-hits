TITLE: Coding Is Solved

STYLE: Neo-soul indie folk hybrid, Gb major, 90 BPM, 12/8 shuffle feel. Warm intimate male vocal — wonder and quiet awe in the voice, not triumphant. Think Hozier's tenderness meets Sampha's electronic soul. Acoustic guitar as the backbone, fingerpicked and unhurried. Sparse programmed beats — finger snaps and a soft kick, no live drum kit. Warm synth pad underneath, barely audible at first. The key sonic signature: a subtle electronic hum or drone weaves in under the acoustic instruments, representing the AI agents running in the background — present but quiet, never louder than the human voice. The song should feel like someone writing in a journal at 2am while machines hum softly in the next room. No electric guitar. No aggressive elements. Bridge strips to voice and synth only. Final chorus swells gently — a held breath, not a shout.

SOURCE: Boris Cherny (Lenny's Podcast)
