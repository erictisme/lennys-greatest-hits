TITLE: Minimum Lovable

STYLE: Intimate acoustic folk, close-mic nylon guitar fingerpicking, warm male vocal (Jonathan Ogden / Jon Guerra warmth), 6/8 waltz feel, 78 BPM, key of Ab major. Soft cello enters on second chorus and swells through bridge. Minimal production — room ambience, felt not heard upright bass on chorus. No drums until bridge (light shaker only). Warm analog recording, like a late-night voice memo that turned into something sacred. Unique sonic element: nylon guitar fingerpicking pattern that feels like a clock ticking forward — progress in every pluck.

SOURCE: Building Lovable: $10M ARR in 60 days with 15 people (Anton Osika, podcast)
