TITLE: The Vibe Coder

STYLE: Dream-pop meets lo-fi electronic, warm synth pads, muted clean electric guitar with chorus effect, programmed beat with finger snaps and 808 rim clicks, 108 BPM, key of Eb major, 4/4. Warm male vocal with slight reverb, conversational and playful delivery. Vocoder harmonies double the hook line "just press send." Light vinyl crackle texture underneath. Tom Misch guitar tone meets Doris Club dreaminess. Bass is round and warm, felt not thumping. Unique sonic element: vocoder-doubled hook and finger-snap rhythm that gives it a futuristic-yet-human feel.

SOURCE: The rise of the professional vibe coder (Lazar Jovanovic, podcast)
