TITLE: Blow Their Socks Off

STYLE: Neo-soul folk-rock build, starts stripped with Rhodes piano and cajón in half-time feel, builds to full band explosion with muted trumpet fanfare on chorus. 118 BPM, key of F# minor, 4/4 with half-time feel on verses. Warm female vocal (soulful, Lianne La Havas energy) with grit on choruses. Rhodes piano bed throughout, clean electric guitar enters on pre-chorus, full drum kit drops on chorus 2, trumpet section (Chet Baker warmth, not bright brass) enters chorus 2 and dominates the finale. Jacob Collier harmonic sophistication meets Hollow Coves cinematic build. Unique sonic element: muted trumpet fanfare that signals each chorus like a starting gun — the sound of a rocket igniting.

SOURCE: Elena Verna 4.0 (Elena Verna, podcast)
