# Research Brief: Blow Their Socks Off

## Source
Elena Verna 4.0
Podcast, 2025-12-18

## Story Arc
Elena Verna is head of growth at Lovable — $200M ARR in under a year with fewer than 100 people. She's led growth at Miro, Dropbox, SurveyMonkey, Amplitude, Netlify. But at Lovable, she's had to throw out 60-70% of everything she ever learned about growth. The old playbook: optimize funnels, smooth friction, A/B test activation flows. The new playbook: reinvent everything, ship features from the growth team, give your product away, and the only thing that actually matters is word of mouth — which you earn by making something so good people can't stop talking about it. "The only way to create a word of mouth loop is just to blow their socks off."

## Specific Numbers & Facts
- $200M ARR in under 1 year
- Fewer than 100 people
- $6B valuation (Series B)
- 8 million users have tried Lovable
- Hundreds of thousands of paid subscribers
- $100M in ~8 months, $200M in ~12 months (doubling in 4 months)
- 95% innovating on growth, 5% optimizing (reverse of her entire career)
- Retention on par with Miro, Dropbox, SurveyMonkey benchmarks
- NDR (net dollar retention) above 100%

## Quotable Lines

## Emotional Core
Exhilaration mixed with vertigo. The feeling of riding a rocket you didn't build and can't fully steer — but you're hanging on with everything you've got. Elena isn't optimizing — she's inventing in real-time. The old maps don't work. The only compass is: make it so good they tell everyone.

## The ONE Thing
In the AI era, growth doesn't come from optimizing funnels — it comes from building something so remarkable people can't shut up about it.
