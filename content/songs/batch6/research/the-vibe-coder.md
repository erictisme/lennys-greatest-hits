# Research Brief: The Vibe Coder

## Source
The rise of the professional vibe coder (a new AI-era job) | Lazar Jovanovic (Professional Vibe Coder)
Podcast, 2026-02-08

## Story Arc
Lazar Jovanovic is the world's first professional vibe coder — a full-time job that didn't exist a year ago. He's never written a line of code. Doesn't want to. Doesn't care what the code looks like. He builds Chrome extensions, merch stores, marketing templates, internal tools — all by prompting. The turning point: someone in the Lovable community said "Chrome extensions aren't possible." Lazar didn't know that. So he just asked Lovable to build one. And it did. His philosophy: come in "positively delusional" — everything is possible until proven wrong. He spends 80% of his time planning and chatting, 20% executing. "We won't be rewarded for faster raw output; we will be rewarded for better judgment."

## Specific Numbers & Facts
- First official vibe coding engineer at Lovable
- Never wrote a single line of code
- 80% planning, 20% executing
- Built: Chrome extensions, merch store, Shopify templates, internal tools, community tools
- Uses Aladdin & Genie analogy for AI prompting
- 36 years old, no technical background
- Community manager built a video generator inside Lovable — before it was a feature

## Emotional Core
Playful defiance. The joy of someone who was told "you can't build that" and did it anyway — not through grit or hustle, but through sheer creative audacity. It's the feeling of a new profession being born, a kid discovering they have superpowers they didn't know about.

## The ONE Thing
The future belongs not to the fastest coders, but to the clearest thinkers. Judgment is the new skill.
