# Research Brief: Minimum Lovable

## Source
Building Lovable: $10M ARR in 60 days with 15 people | Anton Osika (co-founder and CEO)
Podcast, 2025-03-09

## Story Arc
Anton Osika built Lovable — an AI software engineer that takes a text prompt and gives you a working product. In the first 4 weeks: $4M ARR. In the first 2 months: $10M ARR. With just 15 people. Fastest growing startup in Europe, ever. The arc: from a PhD student in Sweden who watched his friends struggle to find developers → renaming GPT Engineer to "Lovable" because "the best word for a great product is that it's lovable" → democratizing software creation for the 99% who don't code. The turning point: they had to throw away their entire codebase and rewrite it while scaling at breakneck speed. And still they grew — purely through word of mouth.

## Specific Numbers & Facts

## Emotional Core
Wonder. The feeling of a door opening that was always locked. The quiet amazement of watching someone who's never coded build something real in 30 seconds. This isn't hustle-bro energy — it's the sacred moment when a dreamer realizes they can actually build the thing.

## The ONE Thing
The best products aren't minimum viable — they're minimum lovable. Build something people can't help but love.
