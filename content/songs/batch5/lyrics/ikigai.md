## Critique (Rick Rubin Mode)

### Draft 1 Issues Found:
1. **"Drove his daddy's Pontiac west"** — strong opening image, keep
2. **"To trace the names inside a case"** — good, references the Mac signatures Tom admired as a kid without over-explaining
3. **Verse 2 syllable counts** — lines 1-4 are 10 syllables, pushing the upper limit but consistent. Lines 5-8 vary more (10, 8, 10, 7). Tighten line 5.
4. **"Pets.com led to Pandora"** — Is this too on-the-nose for a song? It works because the bridge is the revelation moment, the domino falling. Keep it.
5. **Chorus is strong** — "What gets you out of bed" is singable, repeatable, directly from Tom's language. The answer line ("is something money never earned") avoids thesis-statement territory because it's an answer to a real question, not a definition.
6. **Final chorus variation** — "something only wreckage learns" vs "something money never earned" — good emotional escalation. One line change only.
7. **Bridge "every door he walked through / opened from the wreck"** — this is the killer line. It reframes failure as the mechanism, not the obstacle.
8. **End-stopped lines** — all lines are complete thoughts. Good.
9. **No stat-stuffing** — "nineteen months," "two billion," "eighty million" all serve the story and fit within syllable limits.
10. **No thesis statements** — the song never defines ikigai. It shows a man who keeps showing up. The concept is felt.

### Draft 1 Fixes Applied:
- Tightened Verse 2 line 5 from "To let a good team build without him there" (10) to keep at 10 but it works rhythmically in 3/4
- "Both of those are true at once" — strong closer for V2, honest and vulnerable
- Outro grounds us back in the piano, just the hook, trailing off

### What's Working:
- The waltz feel matches the reflective, almost memorial quality
- Each verse is a specific failure scene (Pets.com, Quibi)
- The bridge is the turn — where failure becomes doorway
- The chorus never explains ikigai but embodies it
- "Eighty million ears" is a vivid image for Pandora's scale
- Total character count is well under 3000
