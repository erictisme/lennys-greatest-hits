- **Source Date:** 2025-06-12
- **Key:** E major

- **Time Signature:** 3/4 (waltz)
- **Genre:** Warm reflective waltz — upright piano, strings (violin + cello), intimate baritone

## First Draft → Critique → Rewrite Notes

### First Draft Issues Identified (Rick Rubin Mode)

1. **Verse 1 line 4** — original "Before I'd learned what I was looking for" was a thesis statement. Replaced with "Before the song had found its name" — metaphor for the unnamed calling, shows rather than tells.
2. **Verse 2 line 4** ("A maker hunting for his medium") — direct quote is great, but "A maker" as an opener is flat. Needs more scene.
3. **Chorus line 3** ("Every line I drew, I drew it blind") — "blind" is a strong word but could be misread as negative/careless. The point is he couldn't see the users, not that he was ignorant.
4. **Verse 3** — "billions deep" is slightly stat-stuffing. The pizza box detail is gold though.
5. **Verse 4 line 1** — 12 syllables exactly, tight. "Bounced right off the culture like a stone" is excellent — direct from Bob's friend's language.
6. **Verse 4 line 4** ("Your career is not your life") — this is a direct Bob quote and works as a gut-punch standalone line. Keep it.
7. **Bridge** — the grandmother story is the emotional peak. Good. But "That's the question... every single time" is a thesis statement. Show the weight, don't label it.
8. **Final Chorus variation** — changing "I drew it blind" to "I drew it for" and "never meet" to "finally see" — the shift from blindness to sight is the emotional payoff. Good.
9. **Outro** — clean and resonant. "I found it in the care" is the killer line landing.
10. **Syllable check needed** on all lines.

### Rewrite Decisions

- V1 L4: Replace thesis with a scene continuation — "before the song had found its name" (metaphor for the unnamed calling)
- V2: Rework to keep "hunting for my medium" but ground it in scene
- Chorus L3: Change "blind" to a softer image — "I drew it wide" (open, generous, not knowing who'd receive it)
- V3: Cut "billions deep" — the 30 countries is enough specificity
- Bridge last line: Replace thesis with an image — "You're at the table every time"
- Overall: Verified syllable counts on final version (see below)
