## Chord Progressions

**Verse:** Bbm7 — Ebm7 — Ab7 — Dbmaj7
(Minor ii-V-I with jazz voicings — dark, sophisticated, walking-bass friendly)

**Chorus:** Gbmaj7 — Fm7 — Bbm7 — Eb9
(Descending motion creates inevitability — "this is just how it is")

**Bridge:** Dbmaj7 — Cm7b5 — F7#9 — Bbm7
(The #9 chord is the "Hendrix chord" — adds grit and tension for the price-of-power revelation)

**Why these work:** The Bbm tonality is inherently dark and cinematic. The jazz voicings (maj7, m7, 9) keep it sophisticated without being academic. The walking bass in Bbm creates a noir film score feel that matches the "uncomfortable truth" content perfectly.
