# Elegant Puzzle

**Song:** Elegant Puzzle
**Album:** Deep Work
**Source:** The engineering mindset | Will Larson (podcast)
**Genre:** Lo-fi hip hop groove
**Key:** E major
**BPM:** 78
**Time:** 4/4
**Vocal:** Warm male vocal, conversational flow
**Sonic Signature:** Ticking clock sample as rhythmic backbone — systems thinking as clockwork
**Reference:** Tom Misch groove meets Doris Club lo-fi warmth
**Chord Progression:** Emaj7 — C#m9 — Amaj7 — B7(add13) (i.e., I — vi — IV — V with jazz voicings, chromatic movement in bass)
**Killer Line:** "No way around, just through"

---

[Intro, lo-fi beat with ticking clock]

[Verse 1, conversational]
Servers restart every twelve
Nobody remembers why
Built a model, clean on paper
But the numbers start to lie
Measured twice and then a third time
Then a tenth, then lost the thread
Dashboard full of perfect data
Nothing actually gets fed

[Chorus, relaxed hook]
Tick, tick — no way around
Tick, tick — just through
Tick, tick — keep turning the dial
Till the pieces pull through

[Verse 2, cinematic]
Champagne flutes and sushi platters
Launch night, and the whole site's down
Five of us still in the building
Thirty days to bring it 'round
Python bug, a default value
Simple thing nobody caught
I became the one who stayed here
Only fool who held the thought

[Chorus, relaxed hook]
Tick, tick — no way around
Tick, tick — just through
Tick, tick — keep turning the dial
Till the pieces pull through

[Bridge, stripped to clock and bass]
Use the tools you already own
Boring strategy that works
Every shiny thing you chase
Is a system left in the dirt
The model breaks, the real world speaks
Fix the gap, then go again

[Chorus, final — one line change]
Tick, tick — no way around
Tick, tick — just through
Tick, tick — keep turning the dial
Till the whole thing pulls through

[Outro, clock fading to silence]

**Craft Rules:**
- [x] Show don't tell — servers restarting, champagne at launch, Python bug, sushi while the site burns. No definitions of "systems thinking."
- [x] One killer line — "No way around, just through" (Will's own Uber motto, universally resonant)
- [x] Chorus repeats identically (final chorus changes "the pieces" → "the whole thing" for payoff)
- [x] End-stopped lines — every line is a complete phrase
- [x] No stat-stuffing — "twelve" (hours) and "thirty" (days) are scene-setting, max 2 per verse
- [x] No insider jargon — server restarts, champagne, Python bug all paint pictures without needing context
- [x] No thesis statements — no "systems thinking means" or "strategy is"
- [x] Sing test — open vowels on "through," "around," "dial," "down," "caught," "own"
- [x] Under 3000 chars — ~1300 chars
- [x] Lo-fi hip hop style — short lines (4-9 syl), repetitive "tick tick" hook, conversational delivery
- [x] Rhyme pattern — every 2nd/4th line (why/lie, thread/fed, down/round, caught/thought, works/dirt, speaks/again uses slant)

**What makes this song unique in Batch 5:**
- Only lo-fi hip hop track — distinct from folk, neo-soul, and acoustic songs
- Ticking clock as literal sonic element and metaphorical throughline
- The Digg V4 crash story gives it a cinematic specificity no other song has
- "Tick, tick" hook is the most minimal/repetitive in the batch — designed to stick
