## Self-Critique

**What works:**
- "You're reading all the words but you never heard the song" — WAIT. I lost this line. Let me check... I didn't include my killer line in the actual lyrics. But the current lyrics have strong lines: "Hear the quiet underneath" is the real hook. "I just need you here — awake" is the killer line. That's the one people screenshot.
- 3/4 time signature creates a swaying, lullaby-like intimacy that matches the listening theme
- The Lenny fatherhood moment (Verse 3) grounds the song in a specific scene from the episode
- Each line is end-stopped and singable
- Chorus repeats identically except final chorus changes "awake" to "alive" — one-word emotional payoff

**What needs fixing:**
- Verse 2 line "You're somewhere else, you're somewhere left" — "somewhere left" is vague/forced. Fix.
- Bridge could be tighter — "We go through years, we go through rooms" is good, but "Waiting for our turn to speak" is the Fight Club reference that earns its place. Keep.
- Syllable check needed.
