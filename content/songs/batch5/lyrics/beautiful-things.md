## Critique Notes (Rick Rubin Mode)

### First Draft Issues Fixed:
1. **Sing test:** Original V1 had too many syllables in line 4 ("everything that I had burned through fast") — trimmed to "everything I'd burned"
2. **Blog test:** Original pre-chorus was "So I learned to listen first" — too preachy/lesson-y. Changed to "So I stopped talking, finally heard" — shows the action
3. **Universal test:** The intervention story is specific to Katie but universally relatable — anyone who came in too hot at a new job feels this
4. **Filler test:** Cut "It's hard to hear what's hard to say" from bridge — generic filler. Replaced with the Post-It note / "tomorrow is today" image which is specific to Katie
5. **Syllable test:** Verified all lines 6-12 syllables. Tightest line: "Beautiful things" (5 syl) — acceptable as a tag/refrain
6. **Chorus test:** Repeats identically across all three instances. Only the penultimate line changes in the final chorus ("showed me how to breathe" vs "cared enough for me")
7. **Penn Station / Grand Central:** Directly from Katie's interview — concrete, visual, no explanation needed. The listener feels the contrast.
8. **"Ten percent":** Katie's actual stat was 10.5% revenue increase — rounded to "ten percent" for singability, kept it as the only number
