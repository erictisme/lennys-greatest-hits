# The Hierarchy

**Song:** The Hierarchy
**Album:** Growth Engine
**Source:** Sarah Tavel — The hierarchy of engagement (Lenny's Podcast, 2023-12-27)
**Genre:** Liturgical folk / CityAlight hymn-style
**Key:** B major
**BPM:** 92
**Time Signature:** 12/8 shuffle

---

## The ONE Thing
Build something that gets stronger every time someone uses it. Core action, accruing benefits, self-perpetuating flywheel.

## Killer Line
"Every hand that turns the wheel makes the wheel remember"

## Critique & Revision Notes

**Draft 1 issues identified:**
- Pre-chorus was originally "What if the metric isn't growth?" — "metric" is jargon. Replaced with concrete language.
- Verse 2 originally had "accruing benefits, mounting loss" — direct framework language, thesis-statement territory. Replaced with "you save what you love here / your recipes, your plans" — shows the accruing benefits through specific imagery.
- Bridge originally named three failed products — too much like a list. Trimmed to two and added the universal lesson ("turn your motion into mine").
- Final chorus changes ONE word: "I" → "we" — the network effect made audible.
