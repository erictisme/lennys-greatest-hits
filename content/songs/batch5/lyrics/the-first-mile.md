## Craft Notes

### Self-Critique (Draft 1 issues found and fixed)

**Issue 1: "Don't take it personal" line was 9 syllables but felt clunky.**
- Fixed: Rewrote to "Don't take it personal, it's just" — flows better with the following line as a couplet.

**Issue 2: "That's optimistic at best" — close to thesis-statement territory.**
- Decision: Kept it. It's conversational, not definitional. The user is smirking, not lecturing.

**Issue 3: Bridge could be stronger.**
- Revised from original "It's not that I'm a bad person" to "It's not that I don't care / It's that I haven't learned to yet" — more honest, more human.

**Issue 4: Final chorus variation check.**
- Only the last line changes: "Not where you want me to be" becomes "And I might just stay for free" — emotional payoff. One line. Rule-compliant.
