TITLE: The Power

STYLE: Dark cool jazz, Bbm, 84 BPM, 4/4 with swing feel. Male vocal, cool and knowing, almost noir — low register, intimate close-mic delivery. Muted trumpet as featured instrument (Chet Baker late-night energy meets Tom Misch sophistication). Walking upright bass, brushed drums with swing, sparse piano comping, muted trumpet fills between vocal lines. Warm analog production, smoky room ambience. Dark mood, confessional energy.

SOURCE: The paths to power: How to grow your influence and advance your career (Jeffrey Pfeffer, podcast)

---
