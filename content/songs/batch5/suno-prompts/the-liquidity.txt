TITLE: The Liquidity

STYLE: Acoustic soul in Gb major, 94 BPM, 12/8 shuffle feel. Warm male baritone vocal, soulful and intimate, close-mic delivery. Prominent upright bass groove that flows like water — the bass IS the liquidity metaphor. Fingerpicked acoustic guitar, light shaker percussion (no drum kit), warm piano pad in choruses. Hollow Coves warmth meets Iron and Wine emotional depth. Analog warmth, room ambience, lo-fi vinyl texture. The song should feel like a slow river — steady, deep, never rushing. Build gently into bridge, full warmth on final chorus.

SOURCE: How marketplaces win: Liquidity, growth levers, quality, and more (Benjamin Lauzier, podcast)

---
