TITLE: Make Me Care

STYLE: Cinematic folk-pop ballad, 6/8 time, BPM 88, key of Db major. Hollow Coves warmth meets JVKE emotional piano builds. Warm male tenor vocal, intimate and close-mic. Orchestral strings that swell gradually through the song — sparse pizzicato in verse 1, legato cello in verse 2, full string section by final chorus. Warm piano throughout. No drums until chorus — then gentle brushed snare and shaker. Nylon guitar fingerpicking in verses. Cinematic build. Analog warmth, room reverb.

UNIQUE SONIC SIGNATURE: Strings that build through the entire song like a sparkline — rising and falling with each section, reaching full orchestral swell only at the final chorus. The strings ARE the emotional arc.

[Intro, strings swell slowly, piano enters]

[Verse 1, intimate, close-mic]
You cleared your throat, the room went cold
Thirty faces, arms are crossed
You built the deck, rehearsed the bold
But every word just gets lost

[Pre-Chorus, building]
You think you're center stage tonight
But that was never yours to hold

[Chorus, full strings enter, 6/8 waltz feel]
Make me care
Show me what could be
Paint the world I've never seen
And I'll follow where you lead
Make me care

[Verse 2, warmer]
She took a graph, drew it by hand
Mapped the speeches, found the thread
A rise, a fall, across the land
From what is to what's ahead

[Pre-Chorus, building]
The torch won't light the whole way through
Five feet of dark is all you need

[Chorus]
Make me care
Show me what could be
Paint the world I've never seen
And I'll follow where you lead
Make me care

[Bridge, stripped to piano and cello]
You're reading from the slides
But no one's reading you
The hero's in the chairs
Not standing at the front
Come down, come close, come through

[Chorus, full orchestral swell]
Make me care
Show me what could be
Paint the world I've never seen
And I will follow where you lead
We care

[Outro, strings fade, piano alone]
