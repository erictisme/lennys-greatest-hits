TITLE: Good Strategy

STYLE: Neo-soul jazz-pop, G#m, 102 BPM, 4/4 with a lazy swing feel. Walking upright bass line anchoring the groove. Rhodes electric piano comping with jazzy voicings (add9, maj7, m11). Brushed snare on 2 and 4, finger snaps on offbeats. Warm male baritone vocal, intimate and confident — Jacob Collier ease meets Tom Misch cool. Close-mic, analog warmth, lo-fi vinyl texture. Minimal arrangement — bass, keys, voice, brushes. Bridge strips to just upright bass and Rhodes. Final chorus adds subtle background harmonies. No guitar. No synths.
