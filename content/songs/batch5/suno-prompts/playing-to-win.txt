TITLE: Playing to Win

STYLE: Contemplative acoustic waltz in Eb major, 3/4 time, 84 BPM. Nylon fingerpicked guitar, warm close-mic male baritone, intimate and unhurried. Cello enters on bridge as featured instrument — sustained, emotional, no vibrato at first then deepening. Lo-fi warmth, room ambience, slight vinyl texture. Hollow Coves warmth meets Jon Guerra honest intensity. Gentle crescendo into final chorus with cello swells. Chord movement: Ebmaj7 - Cm9 - Abmaj7 - Bb (verse), Ebmaj7 - Gm7 - Abmaj7 - Bb7sus4 (chorus). No drums until chorus — soft brushed snare on 2 and 3 only. Outro fades to solo guitar.
