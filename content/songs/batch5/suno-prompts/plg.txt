TITLE: PLG (Product-Led Growth)
STYLE: Jazz-pop groove in Ab major, 96 BPM, Tom Misch meets Doris Club. Rhodes electric piano with warm chorus effect, clean jazz guitar comping on offbeats, programmed lo-fi drums with vinyl crackle and side-stick, round upright bass walking quarter notes. Warm male tenor vocal, close-mic, breathy and conversational. UNIQUE SONIC SIGNATURE: a gentle Fender Rhodes tremolo riff that repeats through every chorus like a heartbeat — steady, inevitable, pulling. Finger snaps on beats 2 and 4 in verses. Light shaker joins in pre-chorus. Full band bloom in final chorus with subtle string pad underneath. Mood: cool confidence, a knowing smile, the calm of something that works without you forcing it.
SOURCE: Elena Verna on how B2B growth is changing, product-led growth, product-led sales (Lenny's Podcast)
