TITLE: The OKR

STYLE: Indie folk-pop waltz, 3/4 time, Eb major, 92 BPM. Warm male tenor vocal, close-mic, earnest delivery. Fingerpicked acoustic guitar, warm piano, metronome-like rim clicks on every beat (ticking clock feel). Cello enters on final chorus. Brushed snare on 1, ghost notes on 2-3. Lo-fi warmth, room ambience. Stephen Day meets CityAlight simplicity. Chord voicings: Eb - Cm7 - Ab(add9) - Bb, with Fm9 and Gm7 color on bridge. No synths. Analog warmth throughout.

LYRICS:

[Intro, metronome clicks, soft piano]

Tracked every metric, every slide
The numbers climbed but something died
I couldn't name the reason why
