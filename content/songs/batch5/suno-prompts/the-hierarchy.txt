TITLE: The Hierarchy
STYLE: Liturgical folk hymn, CityAlight-inspired congregational build, 12/8 shuffle, B major, 92 BPM. Warm close-mic male vocal, intimate and earnest. Fingerpicked acoustic guitar foundation, nylon string warmth. Layered vocals build each chorus — verse is solo voice, first chorus adds a second voice in harmony, second chorus adds a third, final chorus is full congregational singing (4-5 voices). Warm analog production, room reverb, lo-fi tape warmth. Light hand percussion (shaker, soft tambourine) enters at pre-chorus. Cello enters at bridge — single sustained note, devastating simplicity. Piano (warm upright, not bright) joins for final chorus. No drum kit. The song should feel like it's being sung in a candlelit room that fills with more and more people.
