TITLE: Elegant Puzzle

STYLE: Lo-fi hip hop groove, E major, 78 BPM, 4/4 straight time. Ticking clock sample as rhythmic backbone layered under dusty boom-bap drum pattern. Vinyl crackle and tape hiss throughout. Warm Rhodes electric piano with jazzy voicings (Emaj7 — C#m9 — Amaj7 — B7add13). Muted electric guitar picking on offbeats, Tom Misch style. Deep sub-bass, felt not heard. Warm male vocal, conversational and unhurried — half-sung half-spoken delivery, Doris Club lo-fi warmth meets Tom Misch ease. Close-mic, analog warmth. Bridge strips to just ticking clock and upright bass. Final chorus adds subtle lo-fi pad swell. No live drums — programmed lo-fi beats only. No synths. No aggressive energy.

SOURCE: The engineering mindset | Will Larson (Carta, Stripe, Uber, Calm, Digg)

---
