TITLE: Ikigai

STYLE: Atmospheric folk waltz, 76 BPM, 3/4 time, C#m. Warm felt piano as anchor, fingerpicked acoustic guitar enters at chorus. Warm male vocal, close-mic, reflective, almost speaking. Subtle strings at bridge. Jon Guerra contemplation meets Bon Iver atmosphere. Lo-fi warmth, intimate room sound. No drums.

Found a song inside the static
