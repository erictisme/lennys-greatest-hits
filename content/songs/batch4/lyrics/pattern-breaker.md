## Critique Notes (Rick Rubin Pass)

**What works:**
- "The coin that can't lose — can't win big" is the quotable moment — direct lift from the source, reframed as a lyric
- "Force a choice — not a comparison / Be the world's first banana" — lands the best Mike Maples phrase as a bridge payoff
- "The slingshot was the part they couldn't see" — shows the inflection/insight framework without naming it
- Pre-chorus 1 sets up the defiant posture cleanly: "Not fighting on your ground"
- Final chorus pivot ("not lonely" replacing "stay lonely") earns its emotional payoff

**What was cut/fixed:**
- Removed VC jargon ("exits," "eighty percent" as a stat line) — replaced with the image of the pivot
- Removed "son" filler from bridge end
- Fixed "I'm changing the whole place" (too abstract) → "I'm finding my own place" (more image-driven)
- Removed "Then build what's breaking through" (clunky) → "Then you build what no one knew" (cleaner close vowels)
