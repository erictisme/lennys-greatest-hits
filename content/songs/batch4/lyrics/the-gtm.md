## Songwriting Notes

**Emotional spine:** Jeanne's 2004 Gmail origin story → 2017 Project Rosland failure (AI wasn't ready) → 2025 same vision rebuilt in 6 weeks for $1,000. The protagonist was right all along — just early.

**Key images used:**
- Gmail's one-year technical lead (2004) — the moment she realized product advantage fades
- Drawing a map of the universe of companies by hand in 2017 — the ambition that failed
- Six weeks / one engineer / $1,000 — the AI unlock of 2025

**Killer line placement:** Bridge — Jeanne's actual quote, delivered at peak emotional intensity after the second chorus. The stomp rhythm drops under it to give the words space.

**What was cut:** The $1M ARR rule, SDR count (10→1), deal-bott, "Vercel builds Vercel with Vercel" — too insider, would require context. The images were kept universal: drawing maps, waiting years, getting back on the diving board.

**Anchor line:** "Touch a customer, make a dollar" — echoes Jeanne's exact GTM definition. Listeners who know the episode will recognize it immediately; listeners who don't will feel its clarity.
