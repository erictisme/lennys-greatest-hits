## The ONE Thing

Free is an acquisition strategy, not a business model — and the reverse trial is the unlock most teams haven't tried yet.

## Killer Line

> "Free is the door — you still need a room"

## Lyric Notes

- **Protagonist:** A product person watching sign-ups flood in, revenue trickle out — wry, knowing, not panicked
- **Verse 1** sets the gap: massive sign-up energy, minimal conversion
- **Verse 2** names the stat (40% never return day one) and introduces the reverse trial concept via "give 'em everything up front / fourteen days before the drop"
- **Bridge** makes the reverse trial emotional: it's about loss aversion, not features
- **Final chorus change:** "Let 'em feel the loss before the thirst" → "Let 'em choose — and now they're asking you" — the payoff: they came back and want in
- Numbers used: 3%, 40%, 5%, 14 days — two per verse max, never stuffed
