TITLE: The GTM
STYLE: Indie folk-rock, BPM 116, key B major, 4/4 time. Warm male baritone, earnest and building — starts intimate, opens to full voice by chorus. Clean electric guitar riff driving the intro and verses, stomping beat, handclaps on the 2 and 4 entering at the pre-chorus. Hollow Coves "Coastline" energy meets Stephen Day warmth. No distortion — clean and purposeful. Bass locks in with kick drum for forward momentum. The bridge strips to voice + electric guitar only before the full band rejoins. Mood: a team marching into battle — confident, earned, not aggressive.
SOURCE: "What world-class GTM looks like in 2026 | Jeanne DeWitt Grosser (Vercel, Stripe, Google)" — Podcast, 2025-11-30
