TITLE: Free to Paid
STYLE: Dream-pop lo-fi, warm female vocal with a playful knowing quality — slight smile in the voice, never ironic. BPM 102, key Db major. Shimmering synth pads layered under programmed lo-fi drums (soft kick, airy hi-hats, gentle snare snap). No acoustic guitar. Warm bass groove, subtle Rhodes chords. Reference: Doris Club dream-pop warmth meets Tom Misch lo-fi groove. Mood: confident and clever, like someone who just found the cheat code. Intimate headphone mix. Occasional gentle synth arpeggio in the gaps between vocal lines.
SOURCE: "Freemium vs. trial" (Newsletter, 2022-03-08), "What is good free-to-paid conversion" (Newsletter, 2023-08-01)
