TITLE: Pattern Breaker

STYLE: Soft synth-pop with folk undertones. E major, 120 BPM, 4/4. Male tenor vocal — breathy and intimate in verses, full-voiced and confident in the chorus. Warm analog synths pulsing on offbeats. Fingerpicked acoustic guitar underneath the verses. Electronic drums with handclaps on 2 and 4. Muted piano accents in chorus. Bridge lifts with layered vocal harmonics and swell. The feel is quietly defiant and visionary — like The 1975 "Somebody Else" meets JVKE emotional piano. Not aggressive. Not anthemic rock. Understated confidence. A thinker's manifesto.

SOURCE: Pattern Breakers: How to find a breakthrough startup idea (Mike Maples Jr., Lenny's Podcast, 2024-07-07)
