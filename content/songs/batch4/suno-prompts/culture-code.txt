TITLE: Culture Code
STYLE: Piano-driven soul waltz in 3/4 time, 96 BPM, Eb major. Grand piano as primary instrument — warm, resonant, close-miked Steinway tone. Subtle orchestral strings enter in chorus (cello + viola, no violin). NO guitar. Warm male tenor vocal, intimate and breathy in verses, building to full-voiced conviction in choruses. Think JVKE "golden hour" piano emotion meets CityAlight hymn-like weight. Brushed cymbal swells on chorus downbeats only. Upright bass (bowed, not plucked) in verses. Production: analog warmth, room reverb on piano, slight tape saturation. Bridge strips to solo piano and voice, strings re-enter for final chorus with added depth. Outro decays to piano and whispered vocal. Elegant, weighty, dignified — a waltz for the workplace.
SOURCE: How Netflix builds a culture of excellence (Elizabeth Stone, podcast)
---
