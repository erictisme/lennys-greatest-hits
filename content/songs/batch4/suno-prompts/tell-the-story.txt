TITLE: Tell the Story
STYLE: Acoustic folk, nylon string fingerpicking, close-mic warm male vocal, conversational and intimate delivery, room ambience with light vinyl warmth, gentle cello entering on bridge, minimal percussion — soft shaker only on choruses, BPM 82, key of G major, Gmaj7 and Cadd9 voicings throughout, Jon Guerra meets Jonathan Ogden energy, unhurried storytelling pace, breathy vocal, slight reverb on voice, analog warmth, the feel of someone telling you something important late at night
SOURCE: The power of strategic narrative (Andy Raskin, podcast)
---
LYRICS:

[Verse 1]
Typed it up in Times New Roman
Mailed it off and held our breath
Red ink came back on the margins
"One out of ten" — he meant the worst
Lower down, three words in cursive
Cut me open at the chest
"Not a compelling story"
And the man was not wrong yet

I stood there half the night
Star Wars is a pitch, I figured —
Trust the Force and choose the good
So I stripped the deck to nothing
Built it back the way a movie would
