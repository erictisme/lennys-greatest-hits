TITLE: Founding Sales
STYLE: Lo-fi neo-soul with jazz-pop warmth. Female vocal, warm and conversational, close-mic intimacy, breathy tone with occasional playful grit. Muted trumpet fills (Chet Baker nod), clean jazz guitar with reverb (Tom Misch energy), Rhodes keys, programmed lo-fi drums with finger snaps on 2&4, vinyl crackle texture, warm upright bass. Half-time feel at 90 BPM in F# minor. Doris Club meets Tom Misch meets Chet Baker. Relaxed, encouraging, slightly playful. Finger snaps, room ambience, analog warmth. Bridge opens up with muted trumpet solo moment. Outro fades with trumpet and vinyl hiss.
SOURCE: Founder-led sales | Pete Kazanjy (Founding Sales, Atrium) (Pete Kazanjy, podcast)
---
