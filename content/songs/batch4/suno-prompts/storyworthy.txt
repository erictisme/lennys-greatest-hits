TITLE: Storyworthy
STYLE: Atmospheric folk, 12/8 shuffle feel, Ab major, 76 BPM. Male falsetto, intimate and aching — Bon Iver meets Jonathan Ogden. Reverb-drenched fingerpicked acoustic guitar, sparse piano entering at chorus, layered vocal harmonies on pre-chorus. No drums until bridge — sparse finger-tap percussion only. Warm late-night production. Slow, contemplative, emotionally restrained. No aggressive energy. Think "Skinny Love" meets "Holocene."
SOURCE: "How to tell better stories | Matthew Dicks (Storyworthy)" — Lenny's Podcast, December 15, 2023
---

Three couples on the lawn
I didn't know they'd come apart

I gave the safe and rounded answers

Before they're even something
