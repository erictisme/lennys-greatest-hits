TITLE: Nobody's Coming

STYLE: Gentle indie rock with forward motion, The 1975 soft synth-pop energy not the heavy stuff, warm male vocal with urgency, clean electric guitar with chorus effect, warm bass, electronic drums with real feel, subtle synth pad, 112 BPM, key of E major, reference The 1975 "Somebody Else" soft energy, reference JVKE emotional builds, scrappy and triumphant mood

SOURCE: How to Kickstart and Scale a Marketplace Business (8-part series) (Lenny Rachitsky + 25 marketplace leaders, newsletter)
