## Chord Progressions

**Verse: G - Bm7 - C - Am7**
The G to Bm7 adds warmth (the 7th softens the minor), C to Am7 creates gentle tension. This progression feels like storytelling around a fire — open, inviting, no rush.

**Chorus: C - D - Em - G**
Starting on C (IV) lifts above the verse energy. D (V) to Em (vi) creates bittersweet tension before resolving to G. The ascending motion matches "carry them higher."

**Bridge: Am7 - D/F# - G - Em**
The D/F# creates a walking bass line between E and G, giving the bridge a sense of journey. More intimate and searching — matches Bob's personal story of overcoming disability through listening.
