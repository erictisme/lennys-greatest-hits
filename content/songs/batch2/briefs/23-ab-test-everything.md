## Chord Notes
- **Verse:** A - E - F#m - D — Classic I-V-vi-IV driving progression. Bright and forward-moving, builds momentum like a team charging into a sprint.
- **Chorus:** D - A - E - F#m — IV-I-V-vi lifts the energy. The D start gives an anthem-like quality that makes "Test everything" feel like a rally cry.
- **Bridge:** F#m - D - A - E — Starting from the relative minor creates tension and wonder. The resolution back to A mirrors the revelation that the "bug" was actually a win.

## Killer Line
"A hundred million from a shrug" — tells the entire Bing story in seven words. Quotable without any context.
