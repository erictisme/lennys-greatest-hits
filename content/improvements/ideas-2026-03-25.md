# Improvement Ideas — 2026-03-25

## Product Context
LGH: 99+ songs across 11 albums, Spotify-aesthetic, contest entry (deadline April 15). Audience: Lenny's PM/founder community. Recent work: dark mode, mobile fixes, 14 new tracks onboarded, 4 QA rounds complete.

---

## Ideas

### Delight

1. **Animated equalizer favicon** — Swap the browser tab favicon to a tiny animated equalizer when music is playing. Users with multiple tabs instantly know music is active. One small file change.
   - Impact: 3 | Effort: 5 (tiny) | Safety: 5 | **Score: 15**

2. **"Related songs" section on track page** — Below "Based on," show 3 tracks that share tags, guest, or album with the current track. Keeps users exploring instead of bouncing.
   - Impact: 4 | Effort: 3 | Safety: 5 | **Score: 60**

3. **Track page quote highlight with accent glow** — The keyQuote blockquote is subtle. Add a very subtle accent-colored glow/shadow behind it to make it the visual anchor of the page.
   - Impact: 2 | Effort: 5 | Safety: 5 | **Score: 10**

### Discovery

4. **"If you liked this" recommendations** — On each track page footer, suggest 3 tracks from different albums that share 2+ tags. Simple tag-overlap algorithm, no ML needed.
   - Impact: 4 | Effort: 3 | Safety: 5 | **Score: 60**
   *(Same as #2 — merged)*

5. **Random "Surprise Me" button** — Next to Shuffle Play on homepage, add a "Surprise Me" button that navigates to a random track page. Lowers the barrier for exploration.
   - Impact: 3 | Effort: 5 | Safety: 5 | **Score: 15**

6. **Topic landing pages with track counts** — The /topics page exists but is basic. Show track count per topic, top track per topic, and make it a browsable discovery surface.
   - Impact: 3 | Effort: 2 | Safety: 4 | **Score: 48**

### Social

7. **Shareable lyric card generator** — On the track page, let users tap a lyric line → generate a styled card image (album art bg + lyric text + track title + "lennys-greatest-hits.vercel.app") they can save/share. Instagram Story format.
   - Impact: 5 | Effort: 1 (complex) | Safety: 4 | **Score: 100**
   *(High impact but high effort — needs canvas/html2canvas)*

8. **"X people are listening now" live counter** — Show a real-time or near-real-time listener count on the homepage. Even "12 people listened today" adds social proof.
   - Impact: 3 | Effort: 2 | Safety: 5 | **Score: 60**

### Content

9. **"New This Week" badge on recently added tracks** — Tracks added in the last 7 days get a small "NEW" badge. Creates urgency and highlights fresh content.
   - Impact: 3 | Effort: 5 | Safety: 5 | **Score: 15**

10. **Album "liner notes" page** — Each album gets a brief editorial note (2-3 sentences) about why these songs were grouped together. Adds depth and intentionality.
    - Impact: 2 | Effort: 3 | Safety: 5 | **Score: 30**

### Conversion

11. **Sticky "Now Playing" mini-bar on track page** — When user scrolls past the player controls on the track page, show a slim sticky bar at the top with play/pause + progress. Prevents losing the player.
    - Impact: 3 | Effort: 2 | Safety: 4 | **Score: 48**

12. **"Listen on Spotify" CTA** — If tracks are on Spotify, add a prominent Spotify link. Drives cross-platform engagement and feels more "real."
    - Impact: 2 | Effort: 5 | Safety: 5 | **Score: 10**
    *(Depends on Spotify availability — skip)*

### Polish

13. **Smooth album cover parallax on scroll** — On album pages, the cover image moves slightly slower than text on scroll. Subtle premium feel.
    - Impact: 2 | Effort: 4 | Safety: 4 | **Score: 16**

14. **Track row hover preview animation** — When hovering a track row, the cover art subtly scales up (1.05x) and the play icon pulses once. Micro-delight.
    - Impact: 2 | Effort: 5 | Safety: 5 | **Score: 10**

15. **Staggered album grid animation** — Albums on homepage already have staggered fade-in. Add a subtle scale (0.95→1) to make it feel more alive on first load.
    - Impact: 2 | Effort: 5 | Safety: 5 | **Score: 10**
