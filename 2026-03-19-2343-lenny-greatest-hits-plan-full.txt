OK here's what I found and my honest answers:

**Suno API — Ralph CAN generate songs overnight:**
- No official API, but there's an open-source wrapper ([gcui-art/suno-api](https://github.com/gcui-art/suno-api)) that works with your Suno Pro cookie
- Deploys to Vercel or runs locally, exposes `/api/generate` and `/api/custom_generate` endpoints
- You'd log into Suno in your browser, copy your session cookie, set it as an env var
- Ralph feeds in lyrics + style prompts, gets back MP3s in ~30 seconds per track
